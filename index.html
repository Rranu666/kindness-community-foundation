Kindness Community Foundation (KCF) is a California nonprofit dedicated to building sustainable community infrastructure for lasting impact. We develop ethical, technology-assisted volunteer networks and transparent governance systems that enable communities to coordinate mutual aid, contributions, and service at scale across the United States.

About Kindness Community Foundation

KCF is building structured, transparent, and technology-assisted community infrastructure that enables lasting impact for neighbourhoods everywhere. Founded in Newport Beach, California, our volunteer-led programs create sustainable systems for collective kindness and mutual aid. We believe every community deserves access to ethical, well-governed infrastructure for social impact.

As a registered nonprofit, KCF is committed to transparent governance, open reporting, and community accountability in everything we do. Learn about nonprofit accountability standards that guide our work.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is a world where every community has access to sustainable, ethical infrastructure for collective kindness and mutual aid, creating lasting impact for generations. Our mission is to promote community stabilization through transparent governance, ethical technology, and structured volunteer coordination that empowers people to serve their neighbours.

Our Key Initiatives

  • KindWave — A real-time community kindness mapping platform connecting people who need help with those who can offer it, creating sustainable ripples of impact.
  • ServeKindness — Structured volunteer coordination for meaningful, trackable community service with lasting impact.
  • Community Giving — Transparent donation infrastructure ensuring every contribution reaches its intended purpose and creates sustainable community benefit.
  • KindWave App — Mobile technology bringing sustainable community support networks to your fingertips for lasting neighbourhood impact.

Leadership & Governance

KCF operates under transparent governance with a volunteer board of directors committed to ethical leadership, community accountability, and sustainable nonprofit practices. Our governance model is designed to ensure lasting impact through clear decision-making, open reporting, and stakeholder engagement.
